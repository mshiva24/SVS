ABOUT

Rural Education and Agriculture Development Society (READS)

is a dynamic and forward looking society. READS is recognised by Government of Karnataka Society Registration act-1960 and approved by the Central Government under the FCR act in New Delhi. With immense strengths and goals READS has started Swami Vivekanand School in 1998-99 with the effort of Founder President Mr. Ashok Patil with a great vision to empower the rural education. Swami Vivekanand School is committed to excellence in teaching and learning. Over the years the Swami Vivekanand School has achieved success by empowering thousands of students with consistent performance, excellent ratings and exceptional awards across its fields.

Vision/Mission

Vision

Our vision to empower rural community by providing quality and affordable education and to enable the young minds to learn life skills to meet the dynamic challenges in life.

Mission

Our mission is to develop young students with active and creative minds, a sense of understanding and compassion for others, and the courage to act on their beliefs. We stress the total development of each child: spiritual, moral, intellectual, social, emotional, and physical.

Chairman's Message

We offer a traditional approach to primary education with a strong emphasis on the core skills of literacy and numeracy. Although we believe in a traditional approach, our lessons reflect modern thinking on how children learn most effectively and our classes and quality staff allow for a very personal approach to learning.
Swami Vivekanand School continues to blossom and grow to serve the needs of rural community students. To help working parents, a private school bus always staffed by our teachers and teaching assistants, runs before and after school from school campus making pick-ups and drop-offs along the route covering all rural places.

I welcome you to have a great learning experience in Swami Vivekanand School.
